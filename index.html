How fast are custom yard signs?
1-3 business days standard. Same-day rush available.
Best material for real estate yard signs?
4mm coroplast with H-stakes. Weatherproof 2+ years.
Political yard signs legal?
Check local election laws. Usually allowed 60 days before election.
Yard sign size standard?
18"x24" most popular. Also 24"x18", 24"x36".
Double sided yard signs needed?
Yes for traffic both directions. Single sided cheaper.
