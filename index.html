Custom Signs Frequently Asked Questions

Custom Yard Signs Questions

How quickly can custom yard signs be produced?
Standard production time is 1-3 business days. Rush service available for urgent real estate and political campaign signage needs.
What material is used for custom yard signs?
Professional 4mm corrugated plastic (coroplast) with UV-resistant ink. Includes standard H-wire stakes. Rated for 2+ years outdoor durability.
What are the standard sizes for yard signs?
Most popular size is 18″ × 24″. Additional sizes available: 24″ × 18″, 24″ × 36″, and custom dimensions.
Are custom yard signs available double-sided?
Yes, double-sided printing available for maximum visibility. Single-sided option available for cost savings.

Vinyl Banners Questions

What vinyl weight is recommended for outdoor banners?
13oz vinyl suitable for 95% of applications. 18oz premium weight recommended for permanent high-wind installations.
How are vinyl banners finished for hanging?
Professional grommet installation every 24 inches around perimeter plus all four corners. Reinforced hem prevents tearing.
What sizes are available for vinyl banners?
Custom sizes from 2′ × 2′ up to 16′ × 50′. Most popular sizes: 3′ × 6′, 4′ × 8′, 5′ × 10′.

Business Signs Questions

What materials are used for safety and business signs?
.040″ aluminum standard for permanent signage. Adhesive vinyl available for temporary applications and equipment labeling.
Are business signs available with reflective material?
Yes, reflective aluminum and high-intensity prismatic options available for parking, safety, and roadway signage.

Custom Stickers Questions

Are custom vinyl stickers waterproof?
Yes, professional outdoor vinyl rated for 3+ years durability. UV resistant and weatherproof construction.
What finishing options are available for stickers?
Contour cut, kiss-cut, sheeted stickers, and die-cut shapes available. Lamination standard for outdoor applications.
